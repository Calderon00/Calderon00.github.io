History

Bluth's Original Frozen Banana is a frozen banana stand started by entrepreneur George Bluth in 1953 as part of the Bluth Company. The Banana Stand is located on the Oceanside Wharf boardwalk on Balboa Island in Newport Beach, California.

In 1963, 10 years after its opening, George Bluth Sr. held a ribbon cutting for the Banana Stand.

In 1970, a musician by the name of Jimmmy Jane released a hit song inspired by the Banana Stand. The song was named, "Big Yellow Joint" due to the Banana Stand's resemblance to a large, yellow marijuana cigarette.

In 2003, Michael and George Michael Bluth purposely burned the Banana Stand down in an act of defiance against George Bluth Sr. who was attempting to continue running the Bluth Company while in prison. Unbeknownst to them, there were $250,000 lining the walls of the Banana Stand.

They rebuilt the Banana Stand shortly thereafter.
